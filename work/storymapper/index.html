Storymapper

A social media app that lets you share stories through AR.

Stories are most often connected to a place and it's always nice to imagine how it happened. Storymapper is a mobile app developed in 3 months, that aims to let people connect through the adventures they have to tell and the places where they ocurred. The augmented reality technology (AR) allows for an avatar to represent each user and tell their stories on the spot where they happened. The focus was to create an interactive social network, where users could add and experience stories in a new and compelling way.

I was mostly involved with the concept design, user research and profiling, prototyping, brand design, development of the app (and AR implementation) and video editing of the demo video for Storymapper.

In collaboration with:
Bruno Carvalho and Sofia Canelas
