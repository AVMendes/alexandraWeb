dat Event

A graphic identity for the event Design, Art and Technology (DAT).

https://avmendes.github.io/uni/dat/

DAT is an event where the areas design, art and technology mix and bring to life creation in innovative ways. The project was to develop the visual identity of the event and that had to convey the idea of union and mix... And so the concept of overlaying elements came to life. There was a focus on generative design and a font with 2 variations was created.

I was mostly involved in the concept design, the creation of the font and the UI and development of the website.

In collaboration with:
José Lopes
