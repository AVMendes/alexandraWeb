Conceptual, Dedicated, Curious.

That's Alexandra Mendes.

I was born on the sunbathed Albufeira, Portugal, and have always been looking beyond the horizon. Expressive and curious, I made the beach rocks my stages and conquered a computer at home.

At 17 years old I became intrigued with the world of design and how an artistic idea could be useful in such subtle ways. It was in Coimbra that I found what I desired, a degree that combined my love for design and technology. But my ambition grew larger and so I joined HYP while I was still a student, where I fell in love with frameworks.

Now that the horizon has stretched a bit more, I want to dive into new opportunities and learn even more as I bring my expertise into the business world.
