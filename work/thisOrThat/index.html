This or That

Oh? A fun & educational game about typography on a website.

https://avmendes.github.io/uni/This_or_That/

This or That was my first web developed game, even though it's made to look like an app. The prompt was to create a digital medium to teach the rules of typography and I decided a game would be a much more fun approach to test, evaluate and teach interested parties.

I was involved with the concept design and website development.
